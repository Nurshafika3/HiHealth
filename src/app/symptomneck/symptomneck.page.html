Symptoms/ Signs

Neck pain is a symptom commonly associated with dull aching. Sometimes pain in the neck is worsened with movement of the neck or turning the head.


Other symptoms associated with some forms of neck pain include:

  • Headache
  • Facial Pain
  • Shoulder pain
  • Numbness
  • Tingling
  • Tenderness
  • Sharp Shooting Pain
  • Range of motion difficulties
  • Fullness
  • Difficulty Swallowing
  • Pulsations
  • Swishing sounds in the head
  • Dizziness/Lightheadedness
  • Lymph node (gland) swelling
