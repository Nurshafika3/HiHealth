Causes of Neck Pain

Example of common causing neck pain:

  • Degenerative disc diseases
  • Neck strain
  • Osteoarthritis
  • Cervical Spondylosis
  • Spinal Stenosis
  • Poor Posture
  • Neck Injury
 Risk factors for Neck Pain:
  • Injury from involvement in contact sports
  • Motor Vehicle accidents
  • Bull or bronco horse riding
